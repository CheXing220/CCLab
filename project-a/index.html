[ Woomipoof ]

Scientific name:

Angels of tears

Discovered by:

Some anonymous sentimental human

Family:

Every human being has a Woomipoof, so the creature has a large family tree. But most people won’t discover the Woomipoof’s existence for their entire life……

Habitat:

A peaceful, sad little creature which only shows up when you’re crying

Appearance:

Half-transparent body, which is constantly fluid like water, and two wings help it to float

Key Characteristics:

  • sad
    • peaceful
  • Has a special mission
    • Can speak human language

What we know so far about Woomipoof? Woomipoof shows up unexpected, but usually born from your tears. Surprisingly, it can talk with you. And it seems that it’s trying to tell you something…… Are you willing to listen to this cute little creature?
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Press keyboard to change the mode of the scene. Press the puddle to summon Woomipoof. Use the tissues to wipe it, and see what will happen.
